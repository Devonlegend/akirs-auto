## Overview

Withholding Tax (WHT) is an advance payment of income tax that is deducted at
source when certain payments are made, and then remitted to the relevant tax
authority. In Akwa Ibom State, the Akwa Ibom State Internal Revenue Service
(AKIRS) collects withholding tax that falls within its jurisdiction, such as
deductions relating to individuals liable to tax in the state. The amount
withheld can typically be credited against the final tax liability of the party
that earned the income. This section explains the general purpose of WHT; the
specific rates, qualifying transactions, remittance procedures, and deadlines
maintained by AKIRS belong in the sections below.
