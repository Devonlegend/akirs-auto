## Overview

The Akwa Ibom State Taxpayer Identification Number (AISTIN) is the unique
identifier that the Akwa Ibom State Internal Revenue Service (AKIRS) uses to
register and track a taxpayer's account with the state. A taxpayer generally
needs this identifier to file returns, make payments, and request services such
as a Tax Clearance Certificate. Both individuals and businesses operating in the
state are expected to be registered. This section explains, in general terms,
what the identifier is for and how the AKIRS Assistant can guide a taxpayer
through registration; the official eligibility, documents, and step-by-step
process belong in the sections below.
