## Overview

Pool Betting and Gaming taxes are levied on operators and activities in the
betting, lottery, and gaming sector within Akwa Ibom State, and are administered
by the Akwa Ibom State Internal Revenue Service (AKIRS) within its jurisdiction.
These taxes generally apply to businesses that run pool betting, lotteries,
casinos, and similar gaming operations in the state. Operators are typically
expected to register, account for the relevant tax on their activities, and
remit it to AKIRS. This section explains the general scope of these taxes; the
official registration requirements, rates, returns, and deadlines belong in the
sections below.
