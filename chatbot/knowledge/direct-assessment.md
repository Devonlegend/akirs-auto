## Overview

Direct Assessment is the method by which the Akwa Ibom State Internal Revenue
Service (AKIRS) assesses the personal income tax of self-employed persons and
individuals in business or trade, as distinct from employees taxed under PAYE.
Under Direct Assessment the taxpayer typically declares their income, and AKIRS
determines the tax due on that income. It applies to people such as sole
proprietors, professionals in private practice, traders, and other individuals
who earn income outside formal employment. This section explains the general
nature of Direct Assessment; the official filing requirements, computation
basis, and deadlines belong in the sections below.
