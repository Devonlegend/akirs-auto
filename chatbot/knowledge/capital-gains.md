## Overview

Capital Gains Tax is a tax charged on the gain that arises when a chargeable
asset is disposed of, for example by sale or transfer. For individuals liable to
tax in Akwa Ibom State, the Akwa Ibom State Internal Revenue Service (AKIRS)
administers the capital gains tax that falls within its jurisdiction. The gain
is generally the difference between what the asset is disposed of for and its
acquisition and related allowable costs. This section explains the general
concept of capital gains tax; the official rate, chargeable assets, exemptions,
computation rules, and filing procedure belong in the sections below.
