## Overview

Pay-As-You-Earn (PAYE) is the system by which employers in Akwa Ibom State
deduct personal income tax from the salaries and wages of their employees and
remit it to the Akwa Ibom State Internal Revenue Service (AKIRS). Under PAYE the
employer is responsible for calculating the correct deduction for each pay
period and for remitting those deductions to AKIRS. This section explains, in
general terms, how PAYE works and how the AKIRS Assistant can help employers and
employees understand their responsibilities. The specific rates, reliefs,
thresholds, and deadlines that apply are maintained by AKIRS and belong in the
sections below.
