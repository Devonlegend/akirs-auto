## What AKIRS is

The Akwa Ibom State Internal Revenue Service (AKIRS) is the agency responsible
for assessing and collecting taxes and revenues due to the Akwa Ibom State
Government. It administers state taxes under the Akwa Ibom State Revenue
Administration Law and applicable Nigerian tax legislation, and it serves
taxpayers, businesses, and residents across the state. The AKIRS Assistant
helps taxpayers understand their obligations, navigate filing and compliance,
and use the service's digital channels.

## Taxes and services AKIRS administers

AKIRS handles a range of state taxes and taxpayer services. These commonly
include Direct Assessment for self-employed individuals, Pay-As-You-Earn (PAYE)
for employees, Withholding Tax (WHT), Capital Gains Tax, and taxes on Pool
Betting and Gaming. AKIRS also issues the Akwa Ibom State Taxpayer
Identification Number (AISTIN), receives annual returns, and issues and
validates Tax Clearance Certificates (TCC).
