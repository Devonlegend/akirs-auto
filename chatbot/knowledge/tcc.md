## Overview

A Tax Clearance Certificate (TCC) is an official document issued by the Akwa
Ibom State Internal Revenue Service (AKIRS) confirming that a taxpayer has met
their tax obligations for the relevant period, or is not liable to tax. A TCC is
commonly requested when dealing with government agencies, applying for contracts
or licences, and in other transactions that require proof of tax compliance.
AKIRS both issues TCCs to compliant taxpayers and provides a way to validate
that a presented certificate is genuine. This section explains the general
purpose of a TCC; the official eligibility, application steps, processing times,
and validation procedure belong in the sections below.
